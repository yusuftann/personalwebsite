Mental Health: Know your mind

Life's not always straight forward is it? It can be further complicated when trying to deal with mental health issues, often on your own. It is important to know what you are experiencing in order to help yourself through it. Many people are not aware of the mental health challenges they face, or do not feel confident and comfortable sharing their situation. This website aims to inform you of the most common types of mental health disorders, with a focus on anxiety. Knowing and naming the problem is the first step in combatting it and regaining control of your mind, and your life, again.
