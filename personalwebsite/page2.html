Anxiety

Anxiety is what we feel when we are worried, tense or afraid â€“ particularly about things that are about to happen, or which we think could happen in the future. Anxiety is a natural human response when we perceive that we are under threat. It can be experienced through our thoughts, feelings and physical sensations.

Physical signs of anxiety could be:headaches / rapid heartbeat / "lump in the throat" feeling / insomnia / excessive sweating / uncontrollable shaking / dry mouth/ hair loss / fast heartbeat

Mental symptoms of anxiety could be: racing thoughts / uncontrollable over thinking / difficulty concentrating / feeling irritable / heightened alertness / changes in appetite / dissociation
